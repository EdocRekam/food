# Country Sausage

Yum

### Ingredients

 - 1/2 tsp Salt
 - 1/4 tsp Pepper, ground
 - 1/2 tsp Sage, rubbed
 - 1/8 tsp Ginger
 - 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
 - 1/4 tsp Thyme
 - 1/4 tsp Paprika
 - 2oz  WATER

### Notes

 - Tried this Aug 2017. It was delicious. I also added a little lemon zest to
   it. Overall it was too salty so I'm cutting the salt in half.
