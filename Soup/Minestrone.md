# Minestrone

This was our first attempt and we love it.

### Ingredients

- 1/8 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
- 1/4 Teaspoon Rosemary
- 1/4 Teaspoon Pepper
- 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoon Thyme
- 1/2 Teaspoon Oregano
- 1/2 Teaspoon Basil
- Chicken Stock
- Hamhock
- Onion
- Carrots
- Celery
- Green Beans
- White Beans
- Navy Beans
- Garlic
- Zucchini
- Cabbage
- Plum/ Italian Canned Tomatoes
- Ditalini Pasta
- Olive Oil

### Directions:
1. Sear ham in olive oil
2. Sweat the onions
3. Deglaze
4. Add everything but pasta and simmer
5. Add pasta
