# DUNCAN HINES CAKE MIX

- 220g Flour
- 200g Sugar
-  16g Powdered Egg (3 Tablespoons)
-  10g Baking Powder (.75 Packet)
- 330g Water
-  80g Oil (.33 Cup)

## INSTRUCTIONS

- Mix at low for ~30 seconds then beat medium 2 min
- Bake @160C for ~40min
