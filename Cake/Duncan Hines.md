# DUNCAN HINES CAKE MIX

- 250g Flour
- 200g Sugar
-  16g Powdered Egg (3 Tablespoons)
-  10g Baking Powder (1 Packet)
- 0.25 Teaspoon Salt
- 330g Water
-  70g Oil (.33 Cup)

## INSTRUCTIONS

- Mix at low for ~30 seconds then beat medium 2 min
- Bake @130C for ~120min

## NOTES

FLAVOR THE CAKE WITH 3 TABLESPOONS OF INGREDIENTS. TRY:

- COCOA
- COCUNUT
- LEMON JUICE
