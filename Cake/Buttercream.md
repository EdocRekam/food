# Genoise Cake

### Base Ingredients

- 3 Egg Yolk
- 120g Sugar
- 250g Butter (Room Temperature)

### Directions

- Beat eggs yolks to goop
- Heat sugar on ban-marie to 120
- Slowly add sugar to eggs
- Whisk for 7 minutes
- Add butter in small increments

### Flavours

- Chocolate Powder
- Vanilla
- Citrus
