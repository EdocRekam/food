# RED VELVET CAKE

### INGREDIENTS

- 220g Flour
- 200g Sugar
- 10g Cocoa
- 1.5tsp Baking Powder
- 0.75tsp Baking Soda
- 0.5tsp Salt
- 180g Milk
- 150g Butter
- 3 Eggs
- 1 tbsp Vinegar
- 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
- Red Food Coloring

### INSTRUCTIONS

- Mix Milk and Vinegar . . . Wait
- Mix Butter + Sugar + Egg (one at a time)
- Add color
- Add Milk + Vinegar (thickened)
- Mix Dry (Sift)
- Bake at 180C for ~30min

### NOTES

- Do not over mix
