# RED VELVET CAKE

### INGREDIENTS

- 220g 110g Flour
- 200g 100g Sugar
- 10g Cocoa
- 1.5 0.75tsp Baking Powder
- 0.75 0.5tsp Baking Soda
- 0.5 0.25tsp Salt
- 180g 90g Milk
- 150g 80g Softened Butter
- 3 2 Eggs
- 1 0.5 tbsp Vinegar
- 2 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
- Red Food Coloring
