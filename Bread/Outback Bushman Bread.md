# Outback Bushmann Bread

We liked this a lot but it still needs some improvement.

### Ingredients

- 355g Water
- 1 Double Espresso
- 30g Butter
- 170g Honey
- 250g Flour
- 250g Wheat Flour
- 20g Sugar
- 15g Cocoa Powder
- 10g Salt
- Fresh Yeast

### Directions

 1. Knead ingredients in machine
 2. Split into loaves
 3. Bake 175C for 20-25 minutes
 4. Sprinkle Corn Meal on top

### Notes

 - Makes 8 loaves
