# Bread Rolls

Fluffy goodness

### Ingredients:

- 100g Water
- 100g Milk
- 76g Butter
- 10g Pepper
- 50g Sugar
- 43g Honey
- 5g Salt
- 450g Pizza Flour
- 11g Fresh Yeast -> 5g Sugar

### Directions:

 1. Mix most stuff in container - heat slightly
 2. Set bread maker to dough
 3. Activate yeast
 4. Mix in bread maker
 5. Bake at 210C
