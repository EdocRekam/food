# Tollhouse Chocolate Chip Cookies

Still experimental, but good

### Ingredients

- 144g Flour (405)
- 75g Butter
- 70g Sugar
- 80g Brown Sugar
- 1 Egg
- 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
- 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
- 150g Chocolate

### Directions
1. Mix ingredients
2. Bake 190C for 9-11 minutes
